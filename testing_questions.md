**Question:** What hardware requirements are needed for the bootcamp?

**Source/Page:** Pages 5, 1, 4, 19, 2, 8, 7, 11

**Answer:**  
The hardware requirements needed for the bootcamp are:   **Operating System:** A current Windows-based laptop with PowerShell or a Mac with Terminal.   
**Processor:** A dual-core Intel i5, AMD 3000+ (CPU), or Apple M1 processor, or greater in power.  
**Memory (RAM):** At least 4 or 8 GB of RAM.   
**Storage:** A solid-state drive (SSD) with at least 256 GB of storage is recommended.   
**Internet Connection:** Recommended minimum speeds are 10 Mbps for download and 3 Mbps for upload.

**Question:** What tutor support is available?

**Source/Page:** Pages 20, 15, 13, 679, 2, 224, 490

**Answer:**  
The following tutor support is available:   Personalized assistance and collaborative coding sessions.   
**Discord Tutoring:** Post coding questions in the designated Discord channel, and a tutor will be promptly assigned to assist.   
**Dedicated Tutor Allocation:** A dedicated tutor will be assigned after orientation to support you throughout the bootcamp, serving as your primary point of contact. Should you want to change your tutor, you can let Suhana (Student Success Manager) know.
